Common Permit Workflows

Building

Home Addition or Alteration

All additions and alterations require a building permit. Depending on scope, sub-permits for electrical, roofing, mechanical, or plumbing may also be required.

Start Permit Guidance
Mechanical

HVAC / Air Conditioning

A mechanical permit is required for all permanently installed AC or heating equipment. Plug-in or portable units do not require a permit.

Get Checklist
Plumbing

Water Heater Replacement

A plumbing permit is required for water heater replacement. An electrical permit may also be needed. Remote final inspection available via Fast Track.

Apply on Fast Track
Zoning

Residential Fence Permit

Fences 6 ft or under require a Zoning permit. Fences over 6 ft or masonry construction require a Building permit. Max 8 ft (side/rear), 4 ft (front yard).

View Requirements
Business

Business Tax Receipt (BTR)

Zoning approval is required before the Tax Collector can issue a BTR. Submit via Fast Track using the Zoning/BZA account category. Contact: (407) 836-5650.

Start BTR Process
Pool

Swimming Pool Installation

A building permit is required for all pools, plus applicable sub-permits for electrical, plumbing, and gas. All applications through Fast Track portal.

Do I Need a Permit?

Requires Building Permit

Structures, Additions & Major Work

New structures (all sizes — even sheds under 120 sq ft still need a permit), additions, alterations, roofing, pools, windows/doors/garage doors, demolition, and any work affecting structural stability.

Sub-Permits Required

Electrical, Mechanical, Plumbing & Gas

Electrical panel upgrades, solar installations, low-voltage work · All permanently installed HVAC/heating (portable units exempt) · Water heaters, plumbing modifications, irrigation systems (underground) · Gas work.

Requires Zoning Permit

Fences, Pavers, Signs & Storage

Fences ≤6 ft · Pavers and concrete work · RV or boat storage · Garage sales (limited to 1 per 6-month period) · Temporary & painted signs · Tree removal on 2+ acre residential lots · Business Tax Receipts (zoning approval first).

No Permit Required

Exempt Work

Residential painting · Plug-in portable appliances/HVAC · Floor covering replacement · Cabinet or countertop replacement (no reconfiguration) · Residential landscaping (excluding irrigation) · Satellite dishes · Flagpoles ≤15 ft.

The 6-Step Permit Journey

1

Training & Prep

Review Fast Track guides, video tutorials, and training resources before starting your application.

2

Plan Preparation

Prepare and submit plans electronically. One electronically signed/sealed set required. No in-person applications accepted.

3

Plan Review

All reviewing agencies review simultaneously via Fast Track. Corrections are uploaded through the portal — not emailed.

4

Permit Issuance

Permit issued through Fast Track. Permits expire 6 months after issuance or last passed inspection. One 90-day extension available.

5

Inspections

Schedule via Fast Track. Remote inspections available for AC change-outs, water heaters, re-pipes, windows & doors final, and more.

6

Final Closure

Pass final inspection to close the permit. Certificate of Occupancy required before occupancy for commercial projects.

All applications at fasttrack.ocfl.net  ·  Help line: (407) 836-8160  ·  Address: 201 S. Rosalind Ave, 1st Floor, Orlando, FL 32802
